Can a coronavirus be prevented or avoided?

How to prevent coronaviruses The best way to prevent the spread of infection is to avoid or limit contact with people who are showing symptoms of COVID-19 or any respiratory infection. The next best thing you can do is practice good hygiene and social distancing to prevent bacteria and viruses from spreading.Practice social distancing. Avoid people who are sick or meeting in large groups. Stay home if you are sick. Cover your cough with a tissue or cough into your upper sleeve or elbow. Do not cough into your hands. Wash your hands often with soap and water for at least 20 seconds, especially after going to the bathroom, before eating, and after blowing your nose, coughing, or sneezing. If soap and water are not readily available, use an alcohol-based hand sanitizer with at least 60% alcohol. Always wash hands with soap and water if hands are visibly dirty. Avoid touching your mouth, nose, or eyes.

Prevention tips

1)Wash your hands frequently for at least 20 seconds at a time with warm water and soap.

2)How long is 20 seconds? About as long as it takes to sing your “ABCs.”

3)Don’t touch your face, eyes, nose, or mouth when your hands are dirty.

4)Don’t go out if you’re feeling sick or have any cold or flu symptoms.

5)Stay at least 3 feetTrusted Source (1 meter) away from anyone who is coughing or sneezing.

6)Cover your mouth with the inside of your elbow whenever you sneeze or cough. Throw away any tissues you use right away.

7)Clean any objects you touch a lot. Use disinfectants on objects like phones, computers, utensils, dishware, and doorknobs.
